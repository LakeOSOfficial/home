AB 1043 Notice

Effective January 1st, 2027, LakeOS will not be available for release to residents of California. AB 1043 is California legislation that impacts software distribution regulations in the state. It requires us OS devs to collect and send age information to all apps who request it, which is a huge violation of privacy. Because of this, we REFUSE to comply with AB 1043, and therefore will not release it to California residents.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this notice or AB 1043, please contact us at lakeosteam@gmail.com
